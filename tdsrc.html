TD Summer Reading Club (TDSRC) is the nation's largest billingual summer reading program aimed for kids of all ages. The program inspires kids to read by allowing them to track the books they have read and want to read, also by having activities where they can participate in trivia, battle of the books, silly stories, and more. Additionally, the site celebrates Canadian content by featuring Canadian artists, authors and stories.
